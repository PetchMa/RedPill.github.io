Mission

Why Did We Tackle This Problem?

The Why.

The average human takes 10,000 hours to master a new skill.That means spending 8 hours a day for nearly 5 years! That’s a lot of work and effort, just for one skill. That’s why humans usually end up only mastering one or at most a couple of skills across their entire lives. But what if you could learn something in a matter of minutes?

The Goal

The goal is simple: to allow people to learn by downloading information into the mind. Our future looks like a world where mastering a new skill is as simple as putting on a cap.

Economic Incestives

The moonshot takes direct aim at the educational sector in the world. The goal is to make skills and education downloadable from digital services.

Take a child for example. Living in a developed country, they would spend over 18 years of their life at school (post-secondary). If we can reduce the time spent a school we can literally unlock years worth of working experience within the youth. Currently the education market is valued at over $4 Trillion USD. RedPill will completely distrupt the education system in the future.

Furthermore, RedPill is aiming to mitigate the effects of technological unemployment (automation) by retraining employees on the fly. Currently on average it costs over $9,000 to retrain a new employee for the job. Imagine being able to lower that cost and accelerate the learning rate.

RedPill will completely change how we will learn in the future.
